Pancakes

As convenient as a store-bought mix can be, every home cook needs a good buttermilk pancake recipe in their repertoire.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt into a large bowl
  2. Beat the buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter together in a separate bowl.
    Do not combine the wet and dry ingredients until right before you cook the pancakes.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven
